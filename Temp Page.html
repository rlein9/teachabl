About Teachabl

We are a new Edtech startup looking to revolutionize the way that teachers search for, gather, and collate materials for usage in their classrooms.

We believe that it can be difficult to find currated, and well organized materials for the classroom.
Many websites are disorganized and it can be difficult to find the exact materials you need.
We want to revolutionize this process and make searching for and gathering materials easier for the average teacher
The founder has experience working in a classroom as a science teacher, and found that it can be difficult to find
the materials you need for your classrom.
